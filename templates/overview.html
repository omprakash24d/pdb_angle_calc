About the Dihedral Angle Calculator

Your tool for calculating Phi (Φ) and Psi (Ψ) dihedral angles from protein structures.

The Dihedral Angle Calculator is a web-based tool designed to calculate Phi (Φ) and Psi (Ψ) dihedral angles for all residues in a protein structure. By providing a .pdb file or a PDB ID, you can calculate these angles and generate a Ramachandran Plot. The results can be viewed in an interactive table or downloaded in CSV, Excel, PDF, and TXT formats. This tool is perfect for researchers, educators, and students involved in protein structure analysis, helping you easily visualize and analyze protein conformations for better scientific insights.
